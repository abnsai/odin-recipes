Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are a fluffy and delicious breakfast staple, perfect with syrup, butter, and fresh fruit.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Baking powder
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Whisk in milk, eggs, and melted butter.
  3. Heat a pan and pour batter onto it.
  4. Cook until bubbles form, then flip.
  5. Cook until golden brown.
  6. Serve with syrup and toppings.
