Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy cheese. Baked to perfection, it is a comfort food favorite.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Italian seasoning

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375Â°F.
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Brown ground beef with garlic and onion.
  4. Add tomato sauce and Italian seasoning to the meat.
  5. Layer noodles, meat sauce, and cheeses in a baking dish.
  6. Bake for 45 minutes.
  7. Let cool before serving.
